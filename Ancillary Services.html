Ancillary Services

Transport Services

Transport may be a good optional service for your events. This may be catered for by the host or just offered at an extra price. Event Planner team may provide you with the vehicle service that you require for any event. This include private taxis, mini buses and coaches, depending on your needs.

Photographer

A photographer is a must in every event. Photos of personal events ensure that memories may last, while in corporate events they may be the main means of showcasing its success. Event Planner team work with photographers who have experience in all the different occassions, ensuring that your final product is better than satisfactory, while still within an affordable price-range.
